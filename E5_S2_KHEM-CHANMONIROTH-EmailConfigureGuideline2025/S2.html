Email Setup Guide

GUIDLINE

Mozilla Thunderbird Setup

  1. Go to thunderbird.net.
  2. Install Thunderbird using the downloaded installer.
  3. Launch Thunderbird and select “Set up an Existing Email Account.”
  4. Enter your name, email (demo@your-name.com), and password.
  5. Choose “Manual config” if automatic setup doesn’t work.
  6. Enter IMAP and SMTP server details.
  7. Click “Finish” to complete the setup.

Microsoft Outlook Setup

  1. Open Outlook or install it from the official website.
  2. Go to “File” → “Add Account”.
  3. Enter your email address (demo@your-name.com).
  4. Enter your password when prompted.
  5. Click “Done” to complete setup.

Gmail "Send As" Setup

  1. Log in to your Gmail account.
  2. Click the ⚙️ icon → “See all settings”.
  3. Go to the “Accounts and Import” tab.
  4. Under “Send mail as,” click “Add another email address”.
  5. Enter your name and demo@your-name.com.
  6. Choose sending method:
    • Send through Gmail (simpler)
    • Send through SMTP (advanced)
  7. Click “Next Step” and verify the code sent to your email.
  8. You're now able to send mail as demo@your-name.com from Gmail.
